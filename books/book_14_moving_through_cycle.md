\# Book 14: Moving Through Your Cycle

\## Aligning Movement with Your Body’s Natural Rhythm



\### Introduction

Your body is not designed to perform the same way every day. Energy rises and falls across your cycle, and movement should follow that rhythm. When you align exercise with your cycle, you reduce burnout, prevent injury, and feel more in control of your body.



\---



\### Chapter 1: Menstrual Phase (Rest \& Restore)

This is when bleeding occurs. Energy is lowest.



Best activities:

\- Stretching

\- Gentle yoga

\- Walking

\- Deep breathing



Avoid:

\- High intensity workouts

\- Heavy lifting



\---



\### Chapter 2: Follicular Phase (Build Energy)

Energy begins to rise after your period.



Best activities:

\- Cardio

\- Trying new workouts

\- Dance

\- Light strength training



\---



\### Chapter 3: Ovulation Phase (Peak Power)

This is your strongest phase.



Best activities:

\- High-intensity workouts

\- Running

\- Team sports

\- Strength training



\---



\### Chapter 4: Luteal Phase (Slow Down)

Energy gradually drops.



Best activities:

\- Moderate workouts

\- Pilates

\- Walking

\- Lower intensity strength



\---



\### Conclusion

Exercise should support your body, not fight it. Move with your cycle, not against it.
