# Book 11: Period Hygiene 101
## Pads, Tampons, Cups, and Care

### Introduction
Period hygiene is essential for comfort, health, and confidence. With so many products available, it can feel overwhelming. This book breaks down the options, explains how to use them safely, and shares stories of women finding what works best for them.

---

### Chapter 1: Pads
Pads are the most common product worldwide. They come in different sizes and absorbencies.  
✨ *Tip:* Change pads every 4–6 hours to stay fresh and prevent irritation.  

---

### Chapter 2: Tampons
Tampons are discreet and allow swimming or sports.  
✨ *Safety:* Always change within 4–8 hours to avoid infection.  

---

### Chapter 3: Menstrual Cups
Reusable cups are eco‑friendly and cost‑effective. They can last up to 10 years with proper care.  
✨ *Story:* *Grace, 27, says: “Switching to a cup saved me money and made me feel more sustainable.”*

---

### Chapter 4: Period Underwear
Modern designs absorb flow and feel like regular underwear. They’re washable and reusable.  

---

### Chapter 5: Hygiene Tips
- Wash hands before and after changing products.  
- Use breathable cotton underwear.  
- Dispose of products properly.  

---

### Conclusion
Period hygiene is about choice. Every woman’s body is different, and the best product is the one that makes you feel comfortable and confident.
