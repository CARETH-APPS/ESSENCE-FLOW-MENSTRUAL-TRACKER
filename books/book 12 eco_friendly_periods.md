# Book 12: Eco‑Friendly Periods
## Sustainable Choices for a Greener Future

### Introduction
Periods don’t have to harm the planet. With billions of disposable products used each year, eco‑friendly alternatives are becoming more popular. This book explores sustainable options and how women worldwide are embracing them.

---

### Chapter 1: The Environmental Impact
Disposable pads and tampons create massive waste. Plastic components can take hundreds of years to decompose. Every month, millions of women contribute to this cycle of waste — but change is possible.

---

### Chapter 2: Sustainable Alternatives
- **Menstrual Cups:** Reusable, long‑lasting, and cost‑effective.  
- **Cloth Pads:** Washable, comfortable, and often handmade.  
- **Period Underwear:** Stylish, discreet, and eco‑friendly.  

✨ *Tip:* Start small — try one eco‑friendly product before switching completely.

---

### Chapter 3: Stories of Change
- *Lina, 22:* “Period underwear gave me freedom and reduced waste. I love that I can wash and reuse them without worrying about running out of supplies.”  
- *Sofia, 19:* “I joined a campus sustainability group, and we all tried menstrual cups together. It felt empowering to make a choice that was good for us and the planet.”  
- *Grace, 27:* “Switching to cloth pads was easier than I thought. I decorated mine with bright patterns, and now they feel personal and fun.”  

---

### Chapter 4: Tips for Transition
- Wash reusable items with mild soap and air‑dry them.  
- Carry a small wet bag for cloth pads when outside.  
- Give yourself time to adjust — every body is different.  

---

### Chapter 5: Global Movements
Organizations worldwide are promoting sustainable menstruation, distributing cups and cloth pads to reduce waste and empower women. Campaigns in schools and communities are helping young girls see eco‑friendly periods as normal and accessible.

---

### Conclusion
Eco‑friendly periods are not just about saving the planet — they’re about reclaiming choice, comfort, and dignity. Every small step makes a difference. By choosing sustainable products, you join a global movement of women who care for themselves and the earth.
