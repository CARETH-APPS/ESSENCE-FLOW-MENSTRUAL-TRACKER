# Book 10: Tracking Your Cycle
## Journals, Apps, and Ancient Wisdom

### Introduction
Tracking your cycle is like learning a secret language your body speaks every month. By paying attention to patterns, you gain insight into your health, emotions, and energy. This book explores modern tools, ancient practices, and personal stories to help you track your cycle with confidence.

---

### Chapter 1: Why Track Your Cycle?
- Predict when your period will start.  
- Understand mood changes and energy shifts.  
- Spot irregularities that may need medical attention.  
- Plan life events around your natural rhythm.  

✨ *Story:* *Ama, 19, says: “Once I started tracking, I realized my best study days always came during my follicular phase. It changed how I plan my exams.”*

---

### Chapter 2: Modern Tools
- **Apps:** Clue, Flo, and other apps make tracking easy.  
- **Wearables:** Smartwatches can monitor temperature and heart rate.  
- **Digital Calendars:** Sync reminders with your phone.  

---

### Chapter 3: Ancient Practices
Before apps, women tracked cycles with moon calendars, beads, or journals. In many cultures, menstruation was linked to lunar phases — the “moon time.”  

---

### Chapter 4: Creative Tracking
- Color‑coded journals.  
- Artistic doodles for moods.  
- Poetry or reflections tied to phases.  

---

### Conclusion
Tracking your cycle is not just about dates — it’s about self‑knowledge. Whether you use an app or a notebook, the goal is the same: understanding yourself more deeply.
